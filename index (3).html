French fries

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Peel the potatoes (optional) and cut them into sticks about 1/4 to 1/2 inch thick.
  2. Fill a deep fryer or a large heavy-bottomed pot with oil to a depth of about 3-4 inches.
  3. Drain the potatoes and pat them dry with paper towels.
  4. Increase the oil temperature to 375°F (190°C).

Thick shake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Recipe

  1. In a blender, combine the ice cream, milk, sugar (if using), and vanilla extract.
  2. Blend the ingredients on high speed until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the thick shake into a tall glass.
  4. Top with whipped cream, a drizzle of chocolate syrup, sprinkles, and a cherry if desired.

virgin mojito

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lime, cut into 4 wedges
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1/2 cup club soda

Recipe

  1. Place mint leaves and 1 lime wedge into a sturdy glass.
  2. Add 2 more lime wedges and the sugar, and muddle again to release the lime juice.
  3. Pour club soda and soda water over the ice.
  4. Garnish with the remaining lime wedge and a sprig of mint.
